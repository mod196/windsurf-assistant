
```powershell

    '-c', 'credential.helper=',
    '-C', $REPO,
    'push', $url, 'main'
) -RedirectStandardOutput $pso -RedirectStandardError $pse -PassThru -NoNewWindow
$p.WaitForExit(120000)
```
